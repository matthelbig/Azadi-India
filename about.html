    Vision

    Azadi is a commitment to improving the quality of life for rural Indian women; by creating spaces in which women can learn, laugh, and connect - Azadi provides women the freedom of choice for them to fulfill their aspirations.

    Mission

    Azadi’s business model is optimized to the rural Indian market; providing an inherent first movers advantage. The key competitive advantage lies in the brands central focus on women: through providing products which are consumer designed and direct-selling through an exclusively female led network, Azadi innovates upon the existing retail process.

    Azadi’s greatest strength is it’s sustainability. By catering exclusively to a female market, and utilizing the powerful human capital there - Azadi promotes the evolution of a community into a network; creating a sustainable landscape for growth.

Where We Work

Azadi works in Uttar Pradesh, India - a state with a global reputation for gender inequity. Uttar Pradesh has over 300,000 rural villages, and is home to millions of women without access to affordable lifestyle products. The strength of community in these villages, combined with the evolving entrepreneurial and economic landscape of India, creates a landscape ideal for the development of a female-distribution network. Furthermore, given the changing social climate and Azadi’s commitment to creating opportunities for women makes Uttar Pradesh a destination for sustainable change.
