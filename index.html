Azadi is a commitment to improving the quality of life for rural Indian women; by creating spaces in which women can learn, laugh, and connect



What is the Problem?

87% of rural Indian retailers are men; this inequity, furthered by cultural sensitivities, creates an environment where women cannot demand nor access their needs.

We Have a Solution

Azadi puts women in the pilot’s seat through developing a network of female entrepreneurs who provide demanded products. Effectively, Azadi breaks the barrier to access; allowing women to ascend to their potential, and celebrate femininity.

Leveraging Community Relationships

Azadi believes in the power of community; by training female social influencers as entrepreneurs, trusted relationships are leveraged into a community distribution network.

Demand Driven Products

Azadi creates products that rural Indian women demand, define, and design; everything Azadi produces is specialized for the rural woman’s wants.

Opening Doors for Aspiration

By eliminating the product access barrier and providing opportunities for women to share in Azadi’s growth; we create a channel where women can open the doors to their own aspirations.
